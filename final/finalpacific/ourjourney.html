Our Journey

Welcome to the Pacific Trails Resort, where passion for the outdoors and adventure await! We encourage you to come experience our favorite place in the world and relax in a peaceful environment. Founded by an Olympic kayaker and nature lover, along with a renowned hiking expert, we are the perfect match for your next adventure. What started as a fun hobby of navigating little waterways in the Pacific Northwest turned into an all-out obsession which led to an Olympic gold medal in kayaking at the 2010 Olympics. My wife, who has been a hiking expert and guide for years has traveled the globe and experienced some of the most epic peaks in the world. Together we specialize in creating an adventure tailored just for you. Whether you are an experienced athlete looking to challenge yourself or the casual weekend warrior who is all about a good time, we will cater to your needs. We have both spent many years in the service industry; we love to treat our guests as family and can't wait to serve you breakfast each morning! Join us in embracing the beauty of nature, one paddle, hike or bird call at a time. We can't wait to see you!
